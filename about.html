What is this?

I had a friend who taught religion at a Catholic school and needed a way to pair her students with a random saint for a project. She hadn't yet found a tool for the job, so I decided to build one for her.

What the app does is generate random pairings from any two lists. I've included two example lists so you can try it out. Feel free to delete them. It should be pretty straightforward, but if you have any questions, please don't hesitate to email me at sam@samfeldstein.xyz

You can also get the app on your phone, but not from the app store. Instead, open the website on your smartphone, tap the share icon , then tap Add to home screen.

The app does not sync between devices because I'm new to software and I don't know how to do that. So any lists you make on your phone won't show up on your computer, and vice versa.

You can read more about this project on GitHub.
